    Rocker line #

    The rocker line is the arch in the board, from tip to tip. The more rocker a board has, the more forgiving it will be. This makes it great for choppy conditions, as it is less likely that the front of the board will dive underwater. This will give it a more freeride experience. A flatter rocker line will make the board faster, as it will plane earlier and thus have less drag resistance. This wThe board will however be more difficult to handle in choppy conditions, and be less forgiving.
